Fish Story

by John Doe

I caught a fish and it was THIIIIIIS BIG! Well, actually, I didn't catch the fish. But longtime Fish Haven resident Dan Smith had a huge turn of luck at the Fish Haven Bear Lake $50k Trout Derby held this spring. This local event is a long-standing tradition for Fish Haven residents and visitors from all around who come together once a year to participate. Smith's angling skill and patience paid off mid-morning when he reeled in the grand prize tagged trout worth $10k! Size of fish aside, now he truly has the biggest fish story of them all! Other tagged trout were caught in the smaller amounts valued at anywhere from $100 to $1000. Be sure to join us next year for this fun-filled community event!
