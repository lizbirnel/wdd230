Don't Feed the Squirrels!

by Suzy Squire

We love our city parks! And we love our city wildlife in the parks! We love our residents enjoying the outdoors with their families. Come and enjoy nature as family and friends in our community, but as a friendly reminder, please do not feed the squirrels. Squirrels are, of course, those cute little animals found in parks all over. They are generally not aggressive, and perfectly wonderful wildlife to have in our green spaces. However, recently some squirrels have become increasing more aggressive and fearless around humans. Why? This is because people are feeding squirrels! Please remember that feeding wild animals, squirrels included, is very bad for them (and us), and as fun as it is, you should stop doing it. Please help us keep our squirrels healthy (our food is not good for them!), help prevent spreading germs (wild animals are just that--wild), and help keep squirrels the calm, peaceful, adorable creatures that they are in our city parks!
