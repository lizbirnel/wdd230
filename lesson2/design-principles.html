Design Principles Document

Elizabeth Birnel

White Space

Apple
https://www.apple.com/


Apple is a good example of this design principle of white space with its clean layout, which adds strong emphasis to the focal point on the page for the user.

Hick's Law

Twitter
https://twitter.com/


Twitter utilizes Hick's Law effectively by creating a specific path for the user to follow with a limited number of choices. This will help increase the length of time the user remains on the page.

Rule of Thirds

Staak
https://www.staak.co.uk/


Staak does a great job with its home page using the Rule of Thirds to help with the design flow on the page. Each of the images used for the home page creates a pleasing aesthetic to the user.
