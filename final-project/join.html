Join the West Jordan City Chamber of Commerce

Member Benefits

Chamber Website and Online Promotion of Your Business
Your business will receive its own webpage hosted through the Chamber site. This is a valuable tool to help drive potential clientele to your own website and business!

Email Marketing and Social Media to Promote Your Business
Your company will be promoted in our Chamber weekly newsletter that goes out every Thursday! This email campaign is a great networking tool and provides more publicity to every member of the Chamber.

Business Resources
The Chamber's Business Resource Center offers resources to all members for ease of funding, finding great employees and trainings, and business communication and client experience to help each business thrive in our economy.

Member Discounts
Receive Promos and share your own promos and discounts with the local business and residential community. These "deals" are posted regularly on the Chamber's homepage of the website, as well as promoted in the weekly newsletter.

Credibility
All members, you included, are Certified and approved by the Board of Directors. This provides a sense of security and encouragement to complete business more with members of the Chamber.
