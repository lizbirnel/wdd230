Current Events

2021 is shaping up to be a great year in West Jordan City! Despite a difficult 2020 season with unforeseen circumstances due to the Covid-19 pandemic, our citizens have rallied and life is going on, even if slightly modified. Businesses have been opened and running and the economy will continue to expand. We're proud to be part of this West Jordan community that works together and will thrive and blossom through trials!

As Spring approaches, get outside and enjoy the sunshine, the hills, the parks, and everything the local community has to offer! The new Wild West Playground is open and ready for families and children to come and enjoy. The local economy is bursting with business and ready to welcome you for all of your recreational, shopping, or other needs. Remember to support local businesses and join with the local Chamber of Commerce to help network and stimulate our local economy, continuing to make West Jordan a wonderful place to live and raise your family!
