Who We Are

We work to connect, grow, and advocate for local businesses in West Jordan, Utah in order to enhance prosperity and growth in the community.

Our Purpose

Business professionals and business leaders who currently engaged in business or would like to conduct business in West Jordan City are encouraged to join the Chamber of Commerce and team up with us. The West Jordan Chamber of Commerce is active in promoting the interests of the local business community in West Jordan City by working to connect local business leaders to potential customers, business partners, or government officials in the local community.
