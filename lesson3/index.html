Site Name:

The site is going to be called Cloudy With a Chance.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve the community with information. From the current weather conditions, to 10-day forecasts, to the updated projections for the season, this site will serve as a resource for the community to be able to rely on in their daily lives. People lead busy lives, and this site will help them know how to prepare for weather conditions at every time of the year.

This site will serve a secondary purpose of increasing the community spirit in the region by listing and promoting community events, sharing relevant news stories, and bringing people together. A community events calendar will be shared with dynamic information for up-to-date activities planned in the local communities for all to enjoy.
