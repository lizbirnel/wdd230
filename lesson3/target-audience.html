Target Audience

The target audience of Cloudywithachance.com is members of the local community, including families and other individuals who are looking for information on weather conditions and how they affect their commutes to work, school, and extra-curricular activities. The site viewers will be middle-aged to older adults who are searching for this local information.

Persona 1: Stacey Young


  • Age: 35
  • Profession: Stay-at-home Mom, Part-time office assistant at elementary school
  • Education: BS in History
  • Family Information: Married 15 years, has 4 kids, 1 boy and 3 girls, aged 14 to 6 years old
  • Motivations for viewing site: As a busy mom, she needs access to current weather conditions regularly. She will visit the site to help plan her day, help select clothing for herself and her children for the week, transport her kids to and from activities by knowing weather conditions, and to access fun, local family events through the community information shared on the site.

Persona 2: Scott Rogers


  • Age: 48
  • Profession: Software Engineer
  • Education: MS in Computer Science
  • Family Information: Married 22 years, father of 3 kids. 1 grown and in college, and 2 teenagers
  • Motivations for viewing site: Commutes daily to work, as well as helps drop kids off at early morning rehearsals and sports practices. Utilizes the site to look up weather forecast to know how much time to plan for commute to work, as well as how to plan for attending high school outdoor sports activities of his children. He also visits the site for score updates for local school sports.

Scenarios

Here are some potential scenarios that Stacey and Scott might face in their day-to-day lives to require using this website.

  • A blizzard is expected to begin tomorrow in the middle of the day. Stacey needs to plan her schedule commuting to pick up her kids at the middle, elementary, and preschool in and around the snow.
  • In early June, the high school swimming team is planning an end-of-season swimming party and Scott needs to know the temperature and weather conditions forecast to help plan the location of the party.
  • A community Easter egg hunt is being planned and details for time, location, and directions are posted on the site.
  • Susan needs to check the weather to help her plan her family's weekend camping trip in the mountains.
  • Scott's college age daughter is driving home for the holidays from Rexburg and he wants to see a forecast to check for safe driving conditions.
