Typograpy

The main fonts used on this sight are Lato and Merriweather.

Heading Font: Lato

This font is a strong sans-serif font whose clean lines and bold structure will provide strong, eye-catching headlines that are easy to read for the viewer.

Body Copy Font: Merriweather

The body copy will be in Merriweather, a pleasing, easy to read serif font that flows well with Lato.
